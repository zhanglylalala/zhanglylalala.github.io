more than fatigue

Word Note
enervate e- (ex-): out
Latin nervus: nerve
not just fatigue, but complete devitalization—physical, emotional, mental
enervation
energy Greek prefix en-: in
ergon: work
erg the term used in physics for a unit of work or energy
synergism prefix syn-: together or with (n might become m when the fist letter of the root is b, m, and p)
the process by which two or more substances or drugs, by working together, produce a greater effect in combination than the sum total of their individual effects
synergistically
synergy an alternate form of synergism

verbal punishment, usually harsh and severe
the strongest word

saying “No!” to oneself

heads and headings

Word Note
captain Latin caput, capitis: head
the head of any group
capital the head city
decapitate to chop off someone’s head
decapitation
recapitulate Latin capitulum: a little head, the heading, or title, of a chapter
to go through the chapter headings again
to summarize or review the main points
recapitulation
recapitulatory the adjection form of recapitulate
capitulate to arrange in headings
to arrange conditions of surrender
capitulation

not the real McCoy

Word Note
simulate Latin similis: like or similar
form similis, derived Latin simulo: to copy
to copy the real thing, pretend to be the genuine article by taking on a similar appearance
simulation
dissimulation you hide your true feelings by making a pretense of opposite feelings
dissimulator
dissemble to hide true feelings by pretending the opposite; or to conceal facts, or one’s true intentions, bydeception; to pretend ignorance of facts you’s rather not admit, when you’re fully aware of them
pejorative word
dissemblance

hints and helps

Word Note
intimate Latin intimus: innermost
intimation contains a significance buried deep in the innermost core, only a hint showing
intimacy
alleviate Latin levis: light
prefix ad-: to
to relieve only temporarily, not to cure or do away with
alleviation
alleviative
elevate to raise out, to raise up
anything light will rise

levitation rising through no visible means
