Intermission

  1. Get has two meanings — namely, obtain and become
  2. Have got can be a substitute for have
  3. Lay means to place, as in “Lay your hand on mine”. Lie means recline
  4. When “neither of” means “neither one of”, “is” is the preferable verb, instead of “are”
  5. A picture is hung, but a person is hanged
  6. The plurals of spoonful and cupful are spoonfuls and cupfuls. You prefer to take in one spoon and filling it more times, instead of using two separate spoons.
  7. Male graduates are alumni while women graduates are alumnae
